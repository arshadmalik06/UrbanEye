Welcome to UrbanEye

            Crowdsourced Civic Issue Reporting & Resolution System A streamlined,
            mobile-first solution designed to bridge the communication gap between
            community members and municipal authorities. This platform empowers 
            citizens to easily report everyday civic issues (like potholes, broken
            streetlights, or uncollected trash) while providing local governments 
            with a powerful dashboard to prioritize,route, and resolve these reports
            efficiently.
